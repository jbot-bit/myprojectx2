## üí° WHY THEY'RE DIFFERENT

### 9AM (Asia Open)
- **First ORB of the day** ‚Üí less conviction
- Needs **HALF stops** to avoid whipsaws
- Needs **high RR** (6R-8R) to overcome low WR
- Essentially a "coin flip with asymmetric payoff"

### 10AM (Mid-Asia)
- **1 hour into session** ‚Üí direction established
- Uses **FULL stops** ‚Üí more confident moves
- **Sweet spot RR: 4R-6R**
- Best overall performer

### 11AM (Late Asia)
- **2 hours into session** ‚Üí moves mature
- Back to **HALF stops** ‚Üí tighter management
- **Lower RR targets** (2R-4R) work better
- Highest win rate (28%)
- Moves are smaller but more reliable
